(A) ** Disaster recovery plan

(B) Offsite backup

(C) Warm site

(D) Fault-tolerant servers

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Create at least three backup copies. Use two different storage media type for backup. Store at least one copy of data offsite.
