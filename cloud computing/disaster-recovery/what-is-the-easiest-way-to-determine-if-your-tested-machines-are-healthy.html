what is the easiest way to determine if your tested machines are healthy?

(A) All machines have undergone initial sync

(B) ** The green line to the left of machine name

(C) No machine undergoing lag

(D) All machines showing “Tested Recently”

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -You run a test failover to validate your replication and disaster recovery strategy, without any data loss or downtime. A test failover doesn’t impact ongoing replication, or your production environment. You can run a test failover on a specific virtual machine (VM), or on a recovery plan containing multiple VMs.

Concept note-2: -Risk Assessment. Evaluate Critical Needs. Set Disaster Recovery Plan Objectives. Collect Data and Create the Written Document. Test and Revise.
