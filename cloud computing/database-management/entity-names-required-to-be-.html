Entity names-Required to be ..

(A) ** descriptive of the objects in the business environment

(B) descriptive of the data represented by the attribute

(C) ** Use terminology that is familiar to the users

(D) Facilitates communication between parties

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -The entity name is the name used by a business to enter into contracts and make other legal or administrative commitments.

Concept note-2: -An entity name can protect the name of your business at a state level. Depending on your business structure and location, the state may require you to register a legal entity name. Your entity name is how the state identifies your business.

Concept note-3: -Examples of named entities are first and last names, geographic locations, ages, addresses, phone numbers, companies and addresses.

Concept note-4: -Named Entity Recognition is a process where an algorithm takes a string of text (sentence or paragraph) as input and identifies relevant nouns (people, places, and organizations) that are mentioned in that string.
