Concept note-1: -Kerberos uses mutual authentication to verify the identity of a user or computer, and the network service being accessed. Each side proves to the other that they are who they claim to be. Kerberos does this through the use of tickets. A Kerberos ticket is encrypted data thatâ€™s issued for authentication.

For example, city and department could be two attributes of users. If both the attributes match, the user is granted access to the object.

Concept note-3: -Password-based authentication Also known as knowledge-based authentication, password-based authentication relies on a username and password or PIN. The most common authentication method, anyone who has logged in to a computer knows how to use a password.

Concept note-4: -Biometrics can help in the granting of this access by authenticating the user in a secure way, such as thumbprint, retina scan, and so on.
