Technically, what is the drawback of cloud computing in industry or other environment?

(A) The absence of hardware

(B) ** The need for high speed internet access

(C) The management of data and products

(D) The wide connection of networks

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Downtime is considered as one of the biggest potential downsides of using Cloud Computing. The cloud providers may sometimes face technical outages that can happen due to various reasons, such as loss of power, low Internet connectivity, data centers going out of service for maintenance, etc.

Concept note-2: -Downtime is often cited as one of the biggest cloud computing disadvantages. Since cloud computing systems are internet-based, service outages are always an unfortunate possibility and can occur for any reason.

Concept note-3: -Data Security and Privacy Data security is a major concern when working with Cloud environments. It is one of the major challenges in cloud computing as users have to take accountability for their data, and not all Cloud providers can assure 100% data privacy.

Concept note-4: -Limited Control. Limited control is generally not a huge issue with cloud services, but it is a potential disadvantage that should be taken into account. When a business moves over to the cloud, users have limited control over the function of their hosting infrastructure.
