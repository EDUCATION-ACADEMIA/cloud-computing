Which are advantages of local hosting?

(A) ** Ideal for local network users-less bandwidth needed

(B) Always on-cannot use sleep mode

(C) ** Costs less than external hosting

(D) Slow bandwidth connection

EXPLANATIONS BELOW

Concept note-1: -Advantages of the in-house web hosting You have full control over access to the website. The choice of hardware, including the ease and expandability of upgrades, is yours. You also have control over the operating environment-the software and systems that run on the web server.

Concept note-2: -Benefits of External Hosting Smaller operations which lack a dedicated IT staff, licensing, additional backup space, internal bandwidth, or time. A company which has power issues in their facility and wants a guaranteed uptime for a geographical office disbursement.

Concept note-3: -The advantages of shared hosting are: A single server’s maintenance costs can be distributed amongst its many users, driving down individual costs to customers. You also have the option to pay more to upgrade to hosting services that allow more traffic, and greater resources.

Concept note-4: -Local server: A server that is managed from its own command line and is hosted on the user’s own personal computer. Cloud server: An infrastructure that stores and processes information and applications belonging to a hosting company, with multiple servers and locations.
